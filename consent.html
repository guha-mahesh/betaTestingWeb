Research Consent Form

Study title: Memory Aid Protocol number: 2 Activation date: 16 Dec 2025

What is the purpose of this research?

We are interested in understanding the mechanisms that govern the encoding and retrieval of memories. For this purpose, you will be presented with personal memories while you are performing different daily tasks and you will be asked to provide feedback on those memories.

Who is conducting this research and where is it being conducted?

This research is being conducted by personnel at Memory Machines. Some tasks may be completed in person and other tasks may be completed online via a web interface.

How are individuals selected for this research study?

Participants older than 18 years of age may participate in this study.

What do I have to do if I am in this research study?

You will be asked to:

  1. (1) Follow instructions to use specific software.
  2. (2) Integrate your digital information. This may include your email, documents and other digital files. The task instructions will describe the type and amount of data required. See engramme.com for more information on the Privacy Policy.
  3. (3) Continue working on your regular tasks while being presented with memory cards.
  4. (4) Provide feedback on the accuracy and usefulness of those memory cards.

We reserved the right to terminate your participation at any time if you are not following the task instructions as defined by our automatic and predefined algorithms.

Are there any risks of this research study?

There are no risks associated with participating in this research study.

There is no guarantee as to the accuracy of the information presented in the memory cards and these memory cards are not meant to provide any form of suggestion or advice to you. They are only presented for you to evaluate their correctness and usefulness. See engramme.com for more information on the Terms of Use.

You can pause and/or stop participating at any time.

Are there any benefits of this research study?

We are performing basic research. There is no direct benefit to you for participating in this study. Some of the memory cards presented could be useful to you. We hope to better understand human cognitive capabilities.

Are there costs associated with this research study?

There are no costs to you associated with participating in this study.

Will I receive any payments?

You will be compensated for your participation. The compensation amount will be posted and communicated to you before you start.

What will happen with the information obtained as part of this research study?

Raw data will be internally used and analyzed for research purposes via algorithms but no one will access any raw personal data. All the feedback that you provide will be analyzed by the researchers. See engramme.com for more information on the Privacy Policy.
