How to Find Your iCloud Email

You need your iCloud email to receive your TestFlight invitation. Here's how to find it on your iPhone.

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.

2. Tap on your name at the top of the Settings screen.

3. Your iCloud email is displayed right under your name. This is the email you need to provide in the eligibility form.

Don't have an iCloud account? You may need to create one at icloud.com.
